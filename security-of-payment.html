Our Service

Quantum and Valuation Expertise for Payment Disputes

Expert Services International assists organisations throughout the construction industry with the identification and analysis of correct quantum and valuation under contract. We provide appropriate analysis, quantification and presentation required for the resolution of security of payment claims or responses across all jurisdictions in Australia.

Payment disputes can escalate rapidly and impact project cash flow and relationships. Our services are designed to provide quick, robust and detailed responses to payment disputes, ensuring your position is properly substantiated and clearly presented to adjudicators, courts and other decision makers.

Why Choose Us

Expert Determination & Adjudication Experience

We can be appointed as adjudicator under security of payment legislation in Western Australia, Northern Territory and New South Wales. This in-depth knowledge of the mechanisms, combined with an understanding of what adjudicators require, allows us to provide rapid, robust and detailed responses for our clients.

Our team brings decades of construction industry experience, ensuring we understand the practical and contractual issues that drive payment disputes. We translate complex financial information into clear, substantiated positions.

Comprehensive Payment Dispute Services

Quick Response Services

Coverage

All Australian Jurisdictions

New South Wales

Comprehensive security of payment services under the Building and Construction Industry Payment Act (NSW). Our team provides adjudication, valuation and claim support across all construction projects.

Queensland

Expert services under Queensland's security of payment legislation. We assist with progress claims, variations and payment schedule disputes across residential, commercial and infrastructure projects.

Victoria

Specialists in Victorian security of payment requirements. Our valuation and claim services ensure compliance with legislation and contractual obligations across all project types.

Western Australia

Accredited adjudicators and valuers under Western Australian security of payment legislation. We provide rapid determination and expert analysis for payment disputes in WA construction projects.

South Australia

Expert security of payment services across South Australian construction projects. We assist with valuations, claims and dispute resolution under South Australian contractual frameworks.

Northern Territory & Tasmania

Accredited adjudicators and experts for Northern Territory security of payment matters. We also provide services across Tasmania and other territories as required.
