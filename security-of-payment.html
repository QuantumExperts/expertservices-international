SECURITY OF PAYMENT

Security of Payment

Security of payment legislation exists in each Australian state and territory to ensure that persons who carry out construction work or supply related goods and services are able to recover progress payments.

Expert Services International provides specialist analysis and support for construction industry security of payment claims. We assist with the preparation of payment claims, payment schedules, and adjudication applications and responses.

Our expertise in quantum, valuation, and construction contract administration enables us to provide comprehensive support throughout the security of payment process, from initial claim preparation through to adjudication.

We have extensive experience with security of payment legislation across multiple jurisdictions, including the Building and Construction Industry Security of Payment Act and equivalent legislation in each state and territory.
