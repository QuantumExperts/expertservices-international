Defect Analysis

Construction Defects

Expert Investigation

Comprehensive Defect Analysis & Assessment

Substandard quality, design issues, and construction defects are common sources of disputes. Expert Services International provides expert investigations examining contract documents, building design, work sequencing, structures, materials, systems, and construction practices. Our thorough forensic approach identifies root causes and quantifies impacts, supporting dispute resolution through expert witness testimony and technical advisory services.

Our Scope

Comprehensive Defect Investigation Services

Review of Contracts, Drawings & Specifications

Detailed examination of contractual requirements, design documentation and technical specifications to establish baseline standards and expectations.

Forensic Analysis of Defect Causes

In-depth investigation to identify root causes of defects, including design inadequacy, material failure, construction methodology and workmanship issues.

Site Inspection & Assessment

Comprehensive site examination and testing to document defects, measure impacts and gather evidence for analysis and reporting.

Quantification of Damage Costs

Expert assessment of remedial costs including material, labour, and associated project impact costs.

Remedial Work Review

Assessment of proposed or completed remedial solutions for adequacy, compliance and cost effectiveness.

Assessment of Secondary Impacts

Analysis of flow-on effects of defects including delays, disruption, acceleration costs and other consequential impacts.

Expert Witness Reports & Testimony

Preparation of comprehensive expert reports and provision of expert witness testimony in litigation, arbitration and other dispute resolution forums.

Expert Approach

Industry Knowledge & Practical Experience

With extensive knowledge and experience across building, engineering, infrastructure and resource industries, Expert Services International is well-positioned to investigate even the most complex construction defect disputes. Our multidisciplinary expertise enables us to assess defects within their full project context, understanding the interaction between design, materials, construction methods and contractual requirements to develop comprehensive, defensible expert opinions.
