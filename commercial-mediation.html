Commercial Mediation

Mediation is a voluntary and confidential process in which a neutral third party assists the parties to a dispute to reach a mutually acceptable resolution. It is a flexible and cost-effective alternative to litigation or arbitration.

Expert Services International provides experienced mediation services for construction and commercial disputes. Our mediators combine technical construction expertise with formal mediation training, enabling them to understand the complexities of the issues at hand and facilitate productive discussions between the parties.

Mediation can be particularly effective for construction disputes where the parties wish to preserve commercial relationships, where there is a desire for a speedy resolution, or where the dispute involves technical matters that benefit from an informed neutral facilitator.

We offer mediation services throughout Australia and internationally, with flexible scheduling and competitive fee arrangements.
