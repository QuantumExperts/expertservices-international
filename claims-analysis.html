CLAIMS ANALYSIS

Claims Analysis & Valuation

TRANSFORMING COMPLEXITY INTO CLARITY

Disputes often arise on projects, and how they are managed can greatly impact outcomes. Drawing on extensive experience with contractual claim analysis, we provide strategic support including quantification, presentation, and assistance with claim resolution.

We help clients determine appropriate actions when claims are identified. Early claim detection provides more options to mitigate potential risks.

With forensic assessment expertise, we can track, identify and respond to large volumes of information that may form the basis of a dispute, establishing the direct costs associated.

Claims Strategy

  • Changes to scope of work
  • Variations
  • Delays and disruption
  • Extensions of time
  • Constructive/directed change
  • Loss of productivity
  • Methodology changes
  • Acceleration
  • Corrective work
  • Site condition changes
  • Management issues
  • Force majeure events
  • Defective contract documents
  • Wrongful termination/suspension
  • Breach of contract

Claims Quantification

We have methodical expertise quantifying costs arising from cost overruns, delays, scope changes, variations, and defective work.
