Comprehensive Services

Construction Claims Analysis

We provide strategic support for the identification, analysis, quantification and presentation of contractual claims. Our expertise covers the full scope of claims management, from early detection through resolution.

01

Claims Identification & Detection

Early identification of potential claims provides critical opportunities to gather evidence, preserve documentation and develop strategy. We assist clients in recognizing situations where contractual entitlements have arisen and where claims should be lodged or preserved. Timely notification and documentation are essential to protecting claim entitlements under most construction contracts.

02

Forensic Assessment & Documentation

With forensic assessment expertise, we can track, identify and respond to large volumes of information that may form the basis of a dispute - establishing the direct costs associated. We organize project records, identify relevant correspondence and financial documentation, and develop chronologies tracing the events that give rise to claims.

03

Strategic Claim Development

We assist in developing claim strategy addressing issues including scope of work changes, variations, delays, extensions of time, productivity losses, acceleration, corrective work and other compensable events. We evaluate the contractual basis for entitlement, assess the strength of the claim and recommend strategy for presentation and negotiation.

04

Claims Quantification

We prepare detailed cost analysis quantifying the financial impact of compensable events. Our analysis addresses both direct costs and consequential costs including preliminary thickening, overhead, financing charges and other extended costs arising from delays or other events.

Claims Expertise

Claims We Analyze & Develop

We have extensive experience across all major types of construction claims. Our strategic support spans the full range of issues that give rise to claims on construction and engineering projects.

Scope Changes & Variations

Analysis of changes to scope of work, evaluation of variation order entitlement and cost impact assessment. We quantify both direct costs and consequential impacts of variations on project performance.

Delays & Extensions of Time

Assessment of delay events, evaluation of extension of time claims and quantification of delay-related costs including extended preliminaries and overhead.

Disruption & Productivity Loss

Analysis of disruption events affecting project productivity, quantification of lost productivity, and assessment of compensability under contractual frameworks.

Acceleration Costs

Quantification of costs arising from directed or constructive acceleration, including overtime, additional resources and expediting costs incurred to recover schedule.

Defective Work & Corrective Action

Analysis of defective work issues and costs associated with corrective action, remediation and rework required to achieve contract compliance.

Site Condition Changes

Assessment of unexpected site conditions, evaluation of impact on construction methods and costs, and quantification of entitlement to compensation.

Management & Administrative Issues

Analysis of management issues including inadequate administration, delay in decision-making and other management failings affecting project performance.

Force Majeure & Suspension Events

Analysis of force majeure events, suspension of work and impacts on project performance and entitlement to compensation or time adjustment.

Contractual Deficiencies

Analysis of defective contract documents including inadequate specifications, ambiguous terms and drawing conflicts affecting project execution and costs.

Breach & Termination

Analysis of contractual breaches, wrongful termination or suspension, and assessment of damages arising from breach of contract.

Financial Analysis

Claims Quantification & Valuation

We have methodical expertise quantifying costs arising from cost overruns, delays, scope changes, variations, and defective work. This requires experienced analysis of initial estimates, schedules, work sequences and progress management to ensure accurate cost assessment.

Cause & Effect Analysis

We establish clear causal relationships between compensable events and financial impacts. Our analysis demonstrates how specific occurrences directly resulted in additional costs, delays or productivity losses. Clear cause-and-effect analysis is critical to establishing entitlement under most construction contracts.

Direct Damages Assessment

We quantify direct costs incurred as a result of compensable events. Direct damages include additional materials, labor, equipment and subcontractor costs directly attributable to the event giving rise to the claim. Our analysis traces these costs to supporting financial records and project documentation.

Prolongation & Extended Project Costs

Where delays extend project duration, we quantify extended costs including site preliminaries, on-site management, supervision, equipment rental, overhead allocation and financing charges. Prolongation costs often represent a significant portion of delay-related claims.

Productivity Loss & Disruption

We assess and quantify productivity losses resulting from disruptions. Our analysis evaluates whether labor productivity was impacted, whether work sequences were disrupted and whether acceleration or additional resource allocation was required to maintain schedule.

Cost Escalation & Inflation

Where projects are extended, we assess cost escalation resulting from inflation. We track labor and material cost increases between original cost estimates and actual construction periods and evaluate entitlement to cost adjustment under contractual frameworks.

Preliminaries & Overhead Thickening

Delays often require continued payment of preliminary costs and overhead allocation over extended periods. We quantify appropriate preliminary thickening reflecting the additional time required for on-site management, facilities and administration.

Presentation & Resolution

Claims Presentation & Management

Effective claims require clear presentation of entitlement, causation and quantum. We assist in preparing comprehensive claims documentation, supporting expert evidence and strategic presentation to support successful resolution.

Claims Documentation & Narrative

We prepare comprehensive claims documentation establishing the factual basis for entitlement. Our narrative clearly describes the events giving rise to claims, establishes contractual entitlement, demonstrates causation and provides a roadmap for supporting financial analysis and expert evidence.

Supporting Financial Analysis

We prepare detailed financial schedules and cost analysis supporting claims. These schedules are cross-referenced to project records and clearly demonstrate the basis for cost quantification. Supporting analysis is prepared in clear, understandable format suitable for presentation to opposing parties or tribunals.

Expert Evidence Support

We prepare expert evidence supporting claims in appropriate cases. Our expert reports address quantum, delay, disruption and technical issues with clear methodology, transparent reasoning and firm conclusions. Expert evidence strengthens claims presentation in formal dispute resolution proceedings.

Negotiated Settlement Support

We support negotiated settlement of claims through provision of clear financial analysis demonstrating the strength of the claim. Credible claims presentations often facilitate settlement discussions and negotiated outcomes superior to formal dispute resolution.

Defense & Rebuttal

We analyze opposing claims and prepare detailed responses and rebuttal analysis. Our defense work addresses the strength of alleged entitlement, challenges the factual basis for claimed causation and examines the reasonableness of damage calculations.

Dispute Resolution Support

We provide ongoing support through mediation, arbitration, expert determination and litigation. Our analysis informs strategy at each stage of dispute resolution and supports clients' efforts to achieve favorable outcomes.
