Formal Dispute Resolution

Arbitration for Construction Disputes

Arbitration is a formal process for resolving disputes that have arisen between parties to a contract. In Australia, federal law governs international arbitration whereas each state has their own laws governing domestic, non-international arbitration. Because of the unique and technical nature of construction projects and the complex relationships involved, appointing a construction arbitrator with broad industry experience and substantial qualifications can assist in the expedient resolution of disputes submitted to arbitration.

Fixed Fee & Low Cost Arbitration
