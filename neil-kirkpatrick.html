Neil Kirkpatrick is the Managing Director of Expert Services International with over twenty years of experience in quantum, technical matters, and delay related to large-scale development and construction projects. His expertise spans building, engineering, infrastructure, marine and resource industries.

Neil holds a Master of Laws in International Commercial Law and a Diploma in International Commercial Arbitration, providing an invaluable combination of technical construction expertise and legal knowledge.

His services cover delay, contract and technical disputes including loss and expense claims, variations, time extensions, liquidated damages and defective work.
