Professional Standards

Adhering to Expert Witness Protocols

Our construction expert witnesses have decades of industry experience and the formal qualifications necessary to provide reliable analysis and testimony. We understand that compliance with codes of conduct established across various legal jurisdictions is essential to serving as a credible expert.

When providing expert investigation, reporting, and testimony internationally, we understand and abide by applicable professional and tribunal codes of conduct, standards and procedural rules. We maintain a thorough knowledge of protocols including those for international arbitration, Australia, the United Kingdom, and the United States.
