The Consultancy

Transforming Complexity Into Clarity

Expert Services International

Expert Services International deliver exceptional standards of independent advisory services to leading legal firms and companies internationally, providing expert witness, dispute resolution and advisory services related to the natural and built environments, resolving complex, contentious and non-contentious issues that arise over the lifecycle of projects, delivering strategic consulting throughout every phase of complex dispute resolution.

We provide premier independent advisory services for natural and built environments, including construction, infrastructure, resources, and engineering industries. Our clients take advantage of the highest standards of advisory services including expert witness, expert determination, arbitration, mediation, strategic advisory services, quantum, delay & disruption analysis, independent dispute resolution, construction claims analysis & advisory services.

Our multidisciplinary expertise, stemming from over three decades of experience, empowers our clients across numerous industries to resolve complex contentious and non-contentious issues relating to all stages of project lifecycles and complex contracts. With extensive experience working with global law firms, developers, owners, employers, contractors, subcontractors and professional practices, we understand our clients' perspectives. This insight, paired with our determination, enables quick solutions tailored to each business' needs. By rapidly comprehending requirements and tenaciously delivering optimal results, we consistently succeed for our clients.
