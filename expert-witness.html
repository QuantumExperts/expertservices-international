Construction Expert Services provides quantum, construction, technical and delay expert investigation, reports and expert witness services associated with the construction and infrastructure industries, natural and built environments. With numerous methods of identifying and analysing quantum and delay, we retain the capability and knowledge based on practical experience as well as robust qualifications and not just impractical theory, avoiding the risk that a report may fail when scrutinised under examination and found to be inaccurate. This service can be key in developing soundly reasoned and factually referenced reports which can be pivotal in delivering a desired financial outcome of a dispute.

Our Credentials

Experienced & Accredited Experts

Experienced in the provision of expert reports and expert testimony and having trained with the Academy of Experts and the RICS, we provide expert witness services associated with the construction and engineering industries that can make the difference in success by conveying the correct expert opinion reflecting an understanding of industry that also embraces factual accuracy. Professional Standards

Adhering to Expert Witness Protocols

When providing expert investigation, reporting, and testimony internationally, we understand and abide by applicable professional and tribunal codes of conduct, standards and procedural rules. Our consultants maintain thorough knowledge of protocols across all major jurisdictions including International Arbitration rules, Australian courts and tribunals, England & Wales civil procedure rules, Hong Kong arbitration frameworks and other established legal systems.

International Compliance Framework

We maintain detailed knowledge of the CIArb Protocol in International Arbitration, IBA Rules on Taking Evidence, and arbitration rules from major institutional seats including Hong Kong, Singapore, Bangkok, Vienna and other centres. Our consultants are trained in the procedural requirements and professional standards applicable to international expert witness work, ensuring that all reports and testimony comply with relevant tribunal rules and codes of conduct.

Australian Court & Tribunal Standards

We comply with expert witness codes of conduct across all Australian jurisdictions including Federal Court, state supreme courts and specialist tribunals. Our experience includes compliance with NSW Schedule 7, Victoria Form 4A, Queensland UCP Rules, South Australian practice directions, WA, ACT and NT protocols, and specialist tribunal requirements including NCAT, VCAT, QCAT and others.

England & Wales & UK Jurisdiction

For matters in England and Wales, we comply with Civil Procedure Rules Part 35 and are familiar with Scottish and Northern Ireland court requirements. Our consultants understand the procedural rules, expert's declaration requirements and duty to the court principles that apply in UK jurisdictions.

Integrity & Independence

We maintain strict professional independence and impartiality in all expert work. Our reports are prepared in accordance with applicable codes of conduct, clearly articulating our qualifications, experience, scope and limitations. We understand the importance of the expert's overriding duty to the court or tribunal and ensure all work reflects this fundamental principle.
