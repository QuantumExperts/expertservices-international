Our Service

Strategic Commercial Consultancy for Construction Projects

From time to time contractual issues that arise on projects can place a burden on a company. Expert Services International provides commercial services that utilise the requisite expertise and experience to resolve issues without detracting from clients' physical resources or disrupting business operations.

Our commercial analysis and consultancy services focus on identifying the root causes of contractual disputes and developing practical, cost-effective solutions that allow your team to focus on core business activities. We bring multidisciplinary expertise in contract interpretation, commercial law, project management, and quantity surveying to resolve even the most complex commercial matters.

Why Choose Us

Multidisciplinary Expertise in Commercial Matters

Our consultants bring extensive qualifications and experience across construction, engineering, legal and commercial domains. We understand the complex relationships between contractual obligations, project management, cost management, and commercial outcomes.

We translate technical and commercial complexities into clear, practical guidance that enables you to make informed decisions and resolve disputes efficiently without prolonged litigation or arbitration.

What We Do

Commercial Consultancy Services

01

Contract Interpretation & Analysis

We analyse complex construction and engineering contracts to clarify obligations, entitlements, and rights. Our interpretation services help parties understand contractual requirements and identify areas of ambiguity or disagreement before disputes escalate into expensive proceedings.

02

Commercial Dispute Resolution Support

We provide strategic support during commercial disputes, helping parties understand their positions, quantify exposure, and develop resolution strategies. Our advisory services address contractual issues including variations, payment disputes, delays, extensions of time, and other commercial matters that arise on projects.

03

Variation & Change Order Management

We advise on the proper process for managing variations, change orders, and variations to scope. Our expertise helps ensure proper documentation, valuation and administration of changes to minimise disputes and protect entitlements throughout the contract lifecycle.

04

Cost & Budget Analysis

We provide detailed cost engineering and budget analysis to support commercial decision-making. Our cost analysis services include evaluating project budget performance, assessing cost impacts of variations and delays, and quantifying additional costs arising from contractual issues.

05

Strategic Advisory on Project Performance

We provide strategic guidance on managing project performance, contractual compliance, and commercial outcomes. Our advisory services help clients navigate complex commercial challenges and make informed decisions that protect their interests while maintaining positive business relationships.
